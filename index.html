About JIET

Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology (JIET) was established in 2003 with an aim to provide Quality Education in Western Rajasthan. It is a leading Institute of Higher Education offering advanced courses in the field of Engineering and Management. The institute is recognized by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated with Bikaner Technical University, Rajasthan.
